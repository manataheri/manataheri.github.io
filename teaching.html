Teaching & Talks

Teaching is close to my heart. I consider it a privilege to be able to share what I know with others, and I do it passionately. I've kept teaching throughout my career, even while working full time. I bring that same enthusiasm to the talks and panels I take part in on a range of topics, gathered here.

Entrepreneurship & Design Thinking

Universidad de La Habana & Humboldt University of Berlin

Since Cuba's 2013 economic reform opened the door to non-agricultural cooperatives, I have taught a course on entrepreneurship and design thinking as part of the International Summer School in Economics and Management Science, a long-standing collaboration between the two universities. Each year, for two weeks, students from Universidad de La Habana and from German universities come together to take classes in economics and management from Cuban and German professors.

Design Thinking Coach

HPI d-school

From 2014 to 2022 I was part of the coaching team at the HPI d-school, where each semester students from different disciplines and backgrounds came together to learn design thinking by applying it to real-world challenges brought in by project partners from the corporate, public, and nonprofit sectors. I coached both the basic and advanced tracks, guiding students through stakeholder management, the problem-solving process, and pitch preparation. I also taught lessons on topics such as user journeys, synthesis, visual thinking, presentation, and storytelling.
