Learning Experience Design

As a learning experience designer, I create programs that don't just transfer knowledge — they change how people work.

My focus isn't on theory for its own sake, but on applicability in each learner's own professional context. Programs are designed to enable behavior change through experiential learning that systematically connects knowledge, experience, and reflection. Methodological variety and clear, audience-appropriate communication are central to how I work.

  • Capability-building programs — multi-session learning journeys that build future-ready skills in teams and across organizations.
  • Train-the-Trainer — equipping facilitators to design and run learning-effective workshops, facilitate group processes, and intervene with intention.
  • Workshop & curriculum design — didactically grounded formats for in-person, online, and blended-learning settings.
  • Digital learning — MOOCs and online courses; I conceived, designed, and ran three MOOCs on human-centered design for the openHPI platform.

How I work

I combine creativity with structure — bringing order to complex topics and designing interactions where learning actually happens. Every program is built around the people in the room and the realities they return to.

Track record

Nearly nine years on the teaching team at the Hasso Plattner Institute, coaching multidisciplinary teams at the School of Design Thinking and leading trainings at HPI Academy for companies and organizations.
