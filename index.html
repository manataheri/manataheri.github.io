About me

Three professions, one profile.

My path runs through strategy, design, and education. I hold a doctorate in Innovation Management and Entrepreneurship (University of Potsdam) and an M.Sc. in Economics and Management (Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin), complemented by a Futures Studies and Speculative Design certificate from Parsons School of Design.

For nearly nine years I was part of the teaching team at the Hasso Plattner Institute — coaching multidisciplinary teams at the School of Design Thinking, leading programs at HPI Academy, and researching digital learning with the HPI–Stanford Design Thinking Research Program. More recently, I led social innovation at Steelcase, designing its first global Better Is Possible Design Challenge.

I bring together strategy, stakeholder engagement, and learning experience design to empower people to innovate with purpose — driven by a belief in design as a tool for equity and impact.
