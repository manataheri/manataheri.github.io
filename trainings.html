Human-Centered Innovation & Digital Prototyping

BI Norwegian Business School & HPI d-school

I designed and led an intensive module for BI's international MBA cohorts from Oslo and Shanghai as part of the school's executive digital track. Over two immersive weeks, teams took an open, real-world challenge from field research and reframing through rapid prototyping and user testing, and on to higher-fidelity concepts built with digital tools and AI-enabled prototyping. I led the program from 2018 to 2022 and, during the pandemic, reimagined it as a fully interactive online experience without losing its hands-on core.

Global Citizenship Program

Melton Foundation & HPI d-school

I designed and led a hybrid program for the Melton Foundation's Global Citizenship Program, enabling Fellows from six universities worldwide to collaborate across cultures on real social challenges. I introduced a project roadmap that gave these distributed teams a clear path to the methods and tools they needed, ways to work together across distance, and a rhythm for sharing progress and gathering feedback from the foundation and their peers. Design, Equity & Inclusion

Mimi Hearing Technologies, EOS, HPI d-school, University of Cape Town

In 2020 I saw an urgent need for a space where designers and makers could take a critical look at their own practice and surface the biases and assumptions they might not notice, in a hands-on, interactive way. The workshop explores the role of design and technology in building a more equitable world. Together we examine privilege and its intersectional nature, and question the comfortable assumption that designers are neutral, well-meaning actors who can do no harm. We look closely at how everyday design decisions include some people and exclude others, and close with a reflection on our own privilege and the power each of us holds to build more inclusive teams and products.
