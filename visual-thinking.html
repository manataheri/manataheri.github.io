Visual Thinking, Illustration & Graphic Recording

Visual thinking is my superpower. I use it to facilitate difficult conversations by externalizing complex ideas and helping teams align, to make innovation projects easier to communicate through illustration, and to capture live conversations as graphic recordings. I also teach professionals and teams to get past their fear of drawing so they can share their ideas with a few simple visuals.

Card Set: Design Thinking Navigator

A set of practical cards for running innovation projects and planning workshops, with a fold-out poster that maps the tools, methods, and mindsets across each phase of the problem-solving process. Visual Thinking and Drawing for Everyone

Visual communication is a crucial skill for everyone, whatever their role or discipline. That's why I run workshops that help professionals and teams overcome their fear of drawing. In these sessions people learn to get their ideas across simply and quickly with a few basic marks, and pick up practical tips for visual facilitation and sketchnoting.
