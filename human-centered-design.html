Human-Centered Design at Scale

How might we teach a hands-on, creative discipline like design thinking online, as an interactive, skill-building experience rather than a series of passive lectures?

I co-designed and ran some of the first skill-based, interactive Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) on the openHPI platform, translating an inherently hands-on methodology into a format learners could practice, not just watch.

We took an iterative approach from the outset, building a “protoMOOC” to prototype and test the learning materials before scaling, running both quantitative and qualitative UX research to sharpen the learner experience, and partnering closely with the platform’s engineering team to ship the new features the format required. Across three courses, the series reached more than 25,000 learners worldwide.
