Innovation & Social Impact

As a consultant and coach, I help teams and organizations innovate with purpose — and scale the initiatives that drive meaningful change.

My approach is context-sensitive and case-oriented. I bring order to complex topics, create structure for dialogue and decision-making, and set impulses that return teams to their collective capacity to act. I structure user-centered development from exploratory research through creative concept development to the piloting of first prototypes.

  • Design Thinking & design sprints — facilitated, human-centered processes that move teams quickly from ambiguity to tested ideas.
  • User research & insight synthesis — exploratory research that turns raw observation into clear, actionable direction.
  • Futures thinking & strategic foresight — exploring plausible futures to make today's strategy more resilient.
  • Social innovation — designing and scaling impact initiatives around education, equity, and environment.

Better Is Possible

At Steelcase, I designed and led the company's first-ever Better Is Possible Design Challenge — launched as a 2023 pilot and expanded to 15 cities worldwide by 2024, engaging 300+ employees, clients, and community partners.

Building the system

I created the program design, training materials, and Train-the-Trainer sessions that enabled global facilitators to adapt the work locally — and led the Better Futures Community, a global learning community on education, equity, and environment.
