Visual Storytelling

I make ideas visible — turning complex conversations into clear, shared pictures that help groups think and decide together.

Visual sense-making sits at the heart of how I work. Whether capturing a live discussion in real time or distilling a strategy into a single image, I balance functionality and aesthetics, working with an eye for detail and audience to give abstract ideas a form people can hold onto.

  • Graphic recording — capturing conversations, conferences, and workshops live, so insight is visible the moment it emerges.
  • Visual facilitation — using imagery to guide groups through complexity toward shared understanding and decisions.
  • Illustration — bespoke visuals that explain, persuade, and make ideas memorable.
  • Learning & toolkit design — workbooks, method sets, and toolboxes that structure, deepen, and sustain learning and consulting processes.

Why it works

A shared picture aligns a room faster than words alone. Visuals externalize thinking, surface assumptions, and give everyone the same reference point — which is exactly what complex, multi-stakeholder work needs.

Design sensibility

With a background spanning design and education, I bring gestalt-confidence to every artifact — balancing clarity, beauty, and function, including in the conception of online courses and blended-learning formats.
